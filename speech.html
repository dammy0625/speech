convert text to speech app

this is a simple app that converts text to speech

this is how to use it

  • input your desired text in the area "write here.."
  • click the "convert text" button to convert your text to speech
  • click the "pause" button to pause the speech
  • click the "resume" button to resume paused speech
  • click the "cancel" button to end/stop all speech
  • the speech will repeat itself the number of time you click the "convert text" button
